THE PREDATOR' Francis Ngannou is the world's hardest hitter, and UFC heavyweight champion

Francis Zavier Ngannou

(born 5 September 1986) is a Cameroonian mixed martial artist. He currently competes in the heavyweight division for the Ultimate Fighting Championship (UFC), where he is the reigning UFC Heavyweight Champion. He is known for being the hardest recorded puncher in the world.[10] As of November 2, 2021, he is #4 in the UFC men's pound-for-pound rankings.
